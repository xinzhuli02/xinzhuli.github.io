About

Graduate student in Information Science at UNC–Chapel Hill, focusing on the ethical design of intelligent systems, inclusive creativity, and digital curation. My research examines how design, metadata, and interaction shape human understanding and agency within technological environments.

Building on my background in archival processing, metadata design, and user-centered information services, I explore how systems can be made more context-aware, transparent, and culturally responsive. My broader interests span information ethics, digital humanities, and human–AI collaboration.
